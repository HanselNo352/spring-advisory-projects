Frequently Asked Questions

How Can I Get a Better Look at the Album Covers on Your Web Page?
You can click the thumbnail images to enlarge them.
How Can I Navigate the Page Faster?
There are a few ways you can navigate the page faster. If you're looking for a specific album, you can click on its name in the "Quick Access Navigation" section at the top of the page and it will direct you to that album on the page. If you need to get to the top of the page you can click on the blue text at the bottom of the page that says "Back to Top". If you're looking to navigate between different pages, you can always go back to the main page from the bottom of the help or contact page.
I Can't See Any of the Images! How Can I Resolve This?
If you're having issues loading the images or audio on the page you can try refreshing or switching to a different browser.
